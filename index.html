About

UNICO. is dedicated to delivering the highest quality, ethically harvested unicorn tears to our charming customers. Although our company is new, our story begins long long ago with our ancestors Zinky and Misty Feathers. In 1775, the sisters were traveling the world when they discovered a lone male unicorn who had been wandering the countryside for years. They helped him reunite with his pack, and then realized these amazing creatures were being forced from their grazing lands. Zinka and Misty knew they had to do something, so they created a new land that the unicorns could call their own.

The refuge they created remains hidden to the average human even today, but it's a wonderous place with many hidden beauties. At the center is a giant lake, which now serves as the source for our unicorn tears. After the unicorns were introduced to their new home, they were so overcome with joy that they cried into the lake, transforming it into an everlasting lake of happy tears.

Although we were set on never trying to capitalize on the magic of our special friends, the head unicorn insisted that we share their gift with the world. In 2017, we began collecting small quanitities from the everlasting lake of unicorn tears, and we hope that your world will be changed by these tears of joy as ours have!

FAQ

Are any unicorns harmed in the making of your products?

No! Our genuine unicorn tears are ethically harvested from wild, free-roaming unicorns! We maintain the utmost respect for our magical friends, and treat each and everyone like family.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes, kind of! We currently ship to every continent, except Antarctica.

Can I visit the unicorn refuge?

Unfortunately, we must keep the location of the refuge secret for the protection of the unicorns. We wish we could share their magical land with all of you, but the risk to their health and wellbeing would be too great.

Where is your headquarters?

Our offices are located in Boulder, Colorado. And no, the unicorn refuge is nowhere near this location.

Where do you get your packaging?

All of our packaging is made from recycled materials collected in the Denver Metro area. In addition to maintaining a safe haven for the unicorns, we also are dedicated to preserving our world as well!
